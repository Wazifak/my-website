About InnoVigyan

What is InnoVigyan?

InnoVigyan = Innovation + Vigyan (Science). It's a place where Science meets Innovation to solve day-to-day problems. We provide an ultimate platform to upskill yourself in a scientific & smarter way.

Vision-Mission of InnoVigyan

  • To create awareness & nourish students in the field of Creativity, Innovation, Memory Championship, Counselling & Life Coaching for overall development.
  • To contribute in educational upliftment with Smart Studies, Teacher training, Career Counselling, technical & Personality grooming oriented programs.
  • To make Studies and Innovation; Fun, Affordable and accessible to all, by providing a platform with proven techniques and right mentorship.
